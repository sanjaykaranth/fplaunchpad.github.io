The [LeanLang for Verified Autonomy Hackathon](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/emergenceai_hackathon-bts-activity-7452702144274661376-4nqk) was a two-day event at IISc Bangalore organised by Prof. Siddharth Gadgil and [Emergence AI](https://www.emergenceai.in/), focused on applying Lean 4 to real-world multi-agent systems.

[Durwasa has a writeup](https://durwasa-chakraborty.github.io/posts/hackathon-query-bridge-at-iisc-bangalore/). He (PhD student) and Vimala Soundarapandian (researcher) entered as team **sambar&Soundness** and took 2nd prize for **QueryBridge**.

QueryBridge uses Lean 4 to formally verify that translating queries between JSON and SQL preserves correctness: proved, not just tested. Congratulations to both.
