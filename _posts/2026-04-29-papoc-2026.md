KC Sivaramakrishnan delivered an invited keynote — *"From Convergence to Confidence: Push-Button Verification for Replicated Data Types"* — at [PaPoC 2026](https://papoc-workshop.github.io/2026/program.html) (13th Workshop on Principles and Practice of Consistency for Distributed Data), co-located with EuroSys 2026 on April 27, 2026. \[[slides](https://kcsrk.info/slides/RDT_verification_papoc_2026.pdf)\]

FP Launchpad members also presented the paper *"Sal: Multi-modal Verification of Replicated Data Types"* (Pranav Ramesh, Vimala Soundarapandian, KC Sivaramakrishnan) at the same workshop. \[[paper](https://kcsrk.info/papers/sal_jan26.pdf) · [explainer](https://fplaunchpad.org/sal/)\]
